King Salmon → Floatplane Handoff
Branch River Air meets you at AKN
After landing in King Salmon, look for Branch River Air either inside the terminal with a sign or in their van in the parking lot. If you do not see them, call (907) 246-3437; the BRA office can contact the van driver.
BRA will drive the group to their river office / floatplane dock, then fly you about 25 miles north to the Alagnak Lodge dock. Flight time is about 20 minutes in the air plus water taxi time at both ends.
Tip from the lodge: arrive in King Salmon on a morning flight and depart on a mid-afternoon flight because BRA handles lodge transfers during the mid-day window.
